# Updates-CIMA-E1-Managing-Finance-in-a-Digital-World-Exam-Guide
The [CIMA E1](https://linkly.link/2ZNHm) (Managing Finance in a Digital World) exam focuses on developing a strong understanding of management accounting, financial strategy, and the role of finance in business decision-making. It covers topics such as financial analysis, performance measurement, budgeting, and the impact of technology on finance.
